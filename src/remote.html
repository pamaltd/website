Remote Monitoring Solution

Features

You can get real-time information about your assets like Generator and Tanks from any part of the world from an internet enabled device.

MONITOR HEALTH AND READINESS

You can access common genset parameters like: Oil pressure, engine temperature, battery voltage, power output, diesel levels, engine run time and engine RPM.

REMOTE OPERATION AND CONTROL

Remotely start and stop your generator in the field from any location. Remotely acknowledge generated alarms. Reduce expensive on-site service visits.

FUEL MANAGEMENT

Receive alarm notification when the fuel level reaches a preset level or fuel theft attempts. Direct alarms to service personnel via SMS or email, RSS feeds.

GENERATOR FLEET MANAGEMENT

Manage large-scale genset rental fleets with in-built GPS functionality. Track the current position of the generator in real-time.

REMOTE CONFIGURATION OF GENSET CONTROLLERS

You can remotely configure a generator module with our solution in combination with the vendor's own utility/configuration software.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?
A communication gateway connects to the power generator using a serial, Ethernet or I/O connection. The gateway sends information via the Internet or the cellular network (GSM/GPRS/3G) to the cloud-based data center. The data can then be access from any internet enabled device.
Can I monitor a mobile Genset?
Yes, you can monitor mobile power generators, making sure that you know the exact location of your asset and that they are always ready to start.
Does this solution works with all Generator Vendors?
Yes, you can monitor gensets equipped with controllers from vendors such as DSE, ComAP, Woodward, DEIF, Caterpillar, FG Wilson, Basler, Cummins, Kohler and many more.
Can I monitor my external tank?
With our Tank Monitoring Solution, you can monitor the content of your tank. This solution gives you the leverage to have real-time data for you current tank volume. This solution is integrated to give you real time alerting via SMS, Email, SNMP and more. The solution gives you the ability and advantage of managing your tank resources, thereby saving energy and money.
